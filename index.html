Abstract

When POSIX system calls are mapped to Scheme exceptions, this small SRFI provides the means to construct, signal, and extract information from those signals in a reasonably uniform way. They are in their own SRFI in order to avoid being tied to any single POSIX-based SRFI such as SRFI 170.
